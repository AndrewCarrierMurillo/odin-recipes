Sour Cucumbers

Sour Cucumbers are a healthy and refreshing treat for a hot summer day.

This recipe uses lemon juice, lemon pepper, and seasoning salt to create the perfect, tart cucumber.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole cucumber
  • 1 cup of fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of Lawry's Seasoned Salt

Directions

  1. Skin cucumber completely.
  2. Slice cucumber width-wise into semi-thick slices.
  3. Juice lemons for 1 cup of lemon juice.
  4. Toss cucumber slices into mixing bowl
  5. Toss seasoned salt, lemon pepper, and lemon juice into mixing bowl.
  6. Mix until spread evenly on cucumbers.
